Everything I Know About Love

“It’s about dealing with growing-up. It’s also very ‘hopeless romantic'. All the songs are based on my personal experiences in the past years, but the way I write about them is like fiction. A lot of these things that we go through in life are a little difficult or bleak, and I try to create magic out of those moments, whether it’s heartbreak, or having a crush on someone, or never having been in love.
