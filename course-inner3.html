Wave To Earth

wave 0.01

This singer shows a lot of his range in his ad-libbing, which I’m all for, and has what I’d classify as a warm, smooth voice. “bonfire” throws you for a bit of a loop, much slower than “gold” – instrumentally, it reminds me a little bit of some of HYUKOH’s tracks. I think I can best describe wave to earth’s sound as atmospheric; every song has a strong sense of depth.
